About me

Hi! I'm Icy Silvertail. You can just call me Icy. Silvertails are a creature of my own creation (you can see a picture on the right).


I'm an artist in my spare time. On this site, you'll find artwork I've created, both on my own and some commissions I've received. Currently, my main medium is digital, using Procreate, but I also do traditional art with graphite pencils, pens, and colored pencils. Sometimes I sketch traditionally and then paint them digitally, I really love doing that!


I also enjoy writing. I have a novel in progress and try to write short stories as often as I can. My dream is to become a published author, although I know that won't put food on the table, haha, but I get exited only thinking about people reading what I write!

(By the way, everything I write is in Spanish, my native language. If I ever translate anything, I'll share it!)
